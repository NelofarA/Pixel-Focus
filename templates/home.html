Your Progress:

The homepage is where you will find your progress here on this website. As you keep making and editing flashcards, attempting quizzes, and focusing using the timer, your progress will be reflected here.

To start making progress, create flashcards.

Skill Cards:

Your skills and work towards those skills are shown here through skill cards. Each card will have the skill's name, accuracy average, and the time spent on the skill.

Accuracy Average:

This is the average score of all your past quiz attempts out of 100%, as shown in the ring below the name. If it shows -1.0%, that just means you haven't attempted a quiz for that skill.

Note the color of the ring is based on your average.

  • If your average is 80% or above, the ring will be green. That's a good standing!
  • If it is between 50% and 79% inclusive, the ring will be yellow. Not bad, but there’s definitely room to improve.
  • If the average is below 50%, then the ring will be red. A sign of failure!

But don’t worry, you can always take more quizzes and boost your average, so keep at it!

Time Spent:

This is the total number of minutes from the focus timer after a session has been completed. This means that incomplete sessions do not count.

For more info on how that works, head to the Focus Timer page.

Note that the default value will be 0 mins upon making a skill.

Level Bar:

This bar shows your progress towards leveling up by getting xp. You can get xp through completing sessions in the focus timer or by getting good scores on the quizzes. Here, you can see exactly how much xp you have, how close you are to leveling up, and what level you are at.

The progress bar will reflect how much xp you have out of 100xp.

Once you have 100xp or more, you level up. Upon leveling up, the progress bar resets to empty and the amount of xp will also reset to 0xp.

Also note that even if you delete flashcards, your xp and level will remain.
