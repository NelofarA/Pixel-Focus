What is this website?

If you have a hard time focusing on a task then is app is for you!

This website is for students to better focus on their studies through flashcards, quizzes, and a timer meant for studying. This is a skills based studying app, input up to 5 skills and start your journey and track your progress on the home page. Earn XP for good scores on quiz section or by completing a study session on the timer.

Feature

  • Flashcards for all you notes
  • Quizzes to test your knowledge
  • Consequences/Anti-game based study timer
